Creamy chicken pasta

Ingredients

Ingredient Amount
Pasta 500 g
Chicken fillet 300 g
Pepper 1 pc
Cream 200 ml
Garlic 2 cloves
Salt and pepper To taste
Oil 1 tbsp

Instructions

  1. Step - fry finely chopped peppers and onions in a large wok pan.
  2. Step - if the onion is glassy, add chicken fillet cut into small pieces.
  3. Step - at the same time boil the pasta with oil and salt.
  4. Step - when the chicken is cooked, flavor it and add the pressed garlic, pour over with cream.
  5. Step - heat until the cream approaches boiling point.
  6. Step - flavor the sauce if needed. Drain the pasta and mix it with the sauce.
